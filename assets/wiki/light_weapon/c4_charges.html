C4 Charges

Sticky demolition charges with a hand clacker. Tap fire to toss a brick — it skids to a stop and sits armed, LED blinking. Hold fire to squeeze the clacker and set off every placed charge in a huge rippling detonation.

Strengths

  • Massive blast radius — the biggest boom a light slot can carry
  • Full control: charges only explode when YOU say so
  • Chain several charges for a rippling minefield trap
  • Fits light and medium weapon slots

Weaknesses

  • Only 5 charges — no resupply
  • No damage at all until detonated
  • Requires planning and baiting enemies onto the charges
