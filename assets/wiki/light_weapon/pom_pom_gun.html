Pom-Pom Gun

Quad-barrel light flak gun straight off a cartoon battleship. Hammers out rhythmic four-round bursts of fat shells that pop into small airbursts at the end of their fuse.

Strengths

  • Airbursting shells deal small area damage even on a miss
  • High burst output against clustered enemies
  • Fits light and medium weapon slots

Weaknesses

  • Belt-rewind pause between bursts
  • Short effective range — shells pop early
  • Limited ammo reserve
