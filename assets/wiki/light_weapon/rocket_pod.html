Rocket Pod

Light multi-rocket platform with modular missile builder support.

Tuning Notes

  • Guidance can be unguided, wire-guided, or homing
  • Fuel and explosive modules reshape speed, lifetime, and damage

Industrial Documentary History

  • 2063: Adopted from aircraft hardpoint pods because ground mechs needed disposable burst fire without turret recoil penalties.
  • 2069: Wire-guided package adapted from demining drones because urban canyons blocked direct line attacks.
  • 2074: Slot-based missile module standard introduced because mixed theater demands required rapid field reconfiguration.
