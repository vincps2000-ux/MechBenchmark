Machinegun

Light rapid-fire weapon for continuous suppression.

Tuning Notes

  • Supports barrel length adjustment
  • Effective when keeping sustained line of fire

Industrial Documentary History

  • 2062: Adopted from convoy pintle mounts because early mech sidearms lacked suppression density.
  • 2066: Receiver simplified using tractor-line castings because remote workshops needed one-tool maintenance.
  • 2070: Feed timing tuned around standardized belts because mixed ammo links caused recurring desync failures.
