Laser

Continuous beam weapon with strong sustained pressure.

Use Case

  • Track moving targets continuously
  • Maintain pressure during retreats

Industrial Documentary History

  • 2067: Adopted from orbital plate-cutting arrays because the command group wanted zero travel time at medium range.
  • 2070: Cooling ring architecture copied from data-center chill loops because first-generation emitters warped after long beams.
  • 2076: Production shifted to sealed modules because dust intrusion caused catastrophic lens cracking in trench biomes.
