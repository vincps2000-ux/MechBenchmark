Chemical Thrower

Close-range spray weapon for area denial and status pressure.

Tuning Notes

  • Switch between fuel, acid, and cryogenics elements
  • Best used while controlling enemy movement lanes

Industrial Documentary History

  • 2064: Adopted from refinery purge lances because infantry flame units lacked pressure consistency in heavy weather.
  • 2068: Acid cartridge option imported from demolition crews because armored husks resisted standard fuel burn.
  • 2072: Cryogenic mix added from cold-chain maintenance systems because squads needed short windows of movement control.
