Plasma Gun

Arc projectile weapon with high burst potential and medium cadence.

Use Case

  • Punish clustered enemies
  • Play around projectile arc timing

Industrial Documentary History

  • 2065: Adopted from foundry induction injectors because shell logistics were too fragile for extended sieges.
  • 2069: Arc stabilizer adopted from rail power substations because random bloom made long-range shots unreliable.
  • 2073: Chamber shielding revised after depot accidents because overpressure spikes cracked pilot canopy armor.
