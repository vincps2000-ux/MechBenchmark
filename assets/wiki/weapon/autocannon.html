Autocannon

Reliable rapid-fire medium weapon with explosive impact feel.

Tuning Notes

  • Supports ammo type changes
  • Supports barrel length adjustments

Industrial Documentary History

  • 2066: Adopted from shipyard anti-drone turrets because existing mech rifles overheated after sustained bursts.
  • 2069: Feed carousel replaced by a belt block borrowed from mining boring rigs because field crews needed faster jam clearing.
  • 2071: Standardized as the workshop baseline because local factories could machine barrels without precision clean-room tooling.
