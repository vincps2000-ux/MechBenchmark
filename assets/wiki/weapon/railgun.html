Railgun

High-impact precision weapon with long cooldown and deep penetration.

Use Case

  • Delete priority targets
  • Punish lined-up enemies

Industrial Documentary History

  • 2068: Adopted from freight launch rails because command needed anti-armor impact without explosive payloads.
  • 2071: Capacitor stack inherited from subway recovery banks because battlefield generators could not meet surge demand.
  • 2078: Barrel laminate switched to layered ceramic-steel because coil pitting reduced accuracy after repeated full-charge shots.
