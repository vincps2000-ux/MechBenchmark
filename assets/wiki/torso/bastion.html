Bastion Torso

Hull-locked assault casemate. The twin guns are welded straight into the sloped glacis — there is no turret traverse at all. Steer the hull to bring the guns to bear.

Strengths

  • Deepest utility bays of any torso (4 utility slots)
  • Two forward-fixed medium weapon mounts
  • Large 4×2 module grid

Weaknesses

  • Zero turret traverse — guns only fire where the hull points
  • Slightly reduced speed
  • Punished heavily by flanking enemies
