Cyclone Torso

Rotary weapon carousel. Two opposed weapon mounts spin continuously on a powered bearing ring — the mech's aim sweeps the full circle whether you like it or not.

Strengths

  • Permanent 360° threat coverage — nothing behind you is safe
  • Two medium weapon mounts firing in opposite directions
  • Excellent with area weapons like flamethrowers and machineguns

Weaknesses

  • Cannot be aimed — mouse aim is ignored entirely
  • Poor with precision weapons that need deliberate targeting
  • Centre spindle blocks the middle module grid cell
